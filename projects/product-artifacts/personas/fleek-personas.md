## Fleek's 3 Customer Segments (From Website)

Fleek explicitly defines 3 buyer segments:

### 1. "Start reselling vintage on Fleek"
**Target:** Beginners, new to reselling
**Value prop:** "Finding your first supplier is often the hardest part of reselling. Fleek makes it easier with access to 1,000+ verified vintage suppliers. Start small with beginner-friendly bundles, test what sells, and grow at your own pace."
**Features:** Small MOQs (10-20 pieces), verified suppliers, buyer protection

### 2. "Scale your reselling business"
**Target:** Full-time resellers with established shops
**Value prop:** "Scaling is tough when supply is unreliable. Fleek helps full-time resellers grow with consistent bulk stock and advanced sourcing tools so you can focus on building your shop."
**Features:** High-demand categories (Levi's 501s, Y2K, sportswear), Demand Hub, Virtual Handpick (video calls, moodboards), supplier relationships

### 3. "Wholesale vintage stock for shops & online stores"
**Target:** Retailers and multi-channel businesses
**Value prop:** "Fleek makes sourcing reliable vintage supply simple for retailers and multi-channel businesses. Connect with 1,000+ verified suppliers, plan seasonal drops with Demand Hub, and handpick bundles that match your shop's aesthetic."
**Features:** Transparent grading, Demand Hub for planning, handpick with suppliers, representative photos
