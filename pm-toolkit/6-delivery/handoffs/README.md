# Handoff Guides

## Purpose
These guides help you prepare product work for handoff to different audiences within your organization. Each guide transforms your product requirements into the format and content that specific teams need.

## Available Handoff Guides

### `to-engineering.mdc`
**Use when:** Handing off for implementation
**What it creates:** Technical specifications with acceptance criteria, edge cases, data requirements, and test scenarios

### `to-design.mdc`
**Use when:** Kicking off design work
**What it creates:** Design brief with user problems, journey maps, constraints, and success criteria

### `to-leadership.mdc`
**Use when:** Seeking approval or updating executives
**What it creates:** Executive summary with business impact, resource requirements, risks, and options analysis

### `to-marketing.mdc`
**Use when:** Preparing for feature launch
**What it creates:** Marketing brief with value propositions, positioning, target audience, and messaging framework

### `to-sales.mdc`
**Use when:** Enabling sales team
**What it creates:** Sales brief with pitch, demo flow, competitive positioning, objection handling, and ROI calculator

### `to-customer-success.mdc`
**Use when:** Preparing for customer rollout
**What it creates:** Support brief with FAQs, troubleshooting guide, training materials, and communication templates

## How to Use

1. **Complete your PRD or core product work first**
2. **Choose the relevant handoff guide** based on your audience
3. **Reference the guide** using `@` in Cursor (e.g., `@guides/handoffs/to-engineering.mdc`)
4. **AI will transform your product work** into the appropriate format
5. **Review and customize** before sharing with the team

## Tips

- **Use multiple handoffs** - One PRD can generate handoffs for engineering, design, marketing, etc.
- **Update as you go** - Handoffs are living documents that evolve with the product
- **Customize for your org** - Edit these templates to match your company's specific needs
- **Link back to source** - Always include links to the original PRD/research

---

These templates save time by ensuring each audience gets exactly what they need in their preferred format.
